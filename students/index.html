A summary of supervision and co-supervision activities @ ISEL/IPL

Over the years, I supervised or co-supervised more than 70 students across Master’s and Bachelor’s programs (MEIC, MEET, LEIC, LEIRT, and LEIM). These works span topics such as distributed systems, cloud and edge computing, cybersecurity, IoT, mobile devices, often resulting in publications and applied research outcomes.
